Simple Process

How Rental Car Excess Insurance Works

From quote to claim — the complete picture in 8 simple steps.

Phase 1 — Before Your Rental
1
Get Your Quote Online

Enter where you're hiring, your rental dates, and click "Get a Quote". The whole process takes under 2 minutes. No credit card, no commitment at this stage.

No price is shown until you submit your quote — your price is calculated and revealed on the next screen only.
2
Choose Your Coverage Level

Select from $4,000 to $8,000 of protection. All tiers include $0 excess — the higher the coverage limit, the more you're protected on high-value or luxury vehicles.

$4,000 — $9.96/day$6,000 — $14.42/day$8,000 — $16.79/day
3
Pay Securely & Receive Your Policy

Complete secure checkout. Your policy document is emailed to you instantly. Save it to your phone or print it — you're now covered.

You don't need to show this at the rental counter. Just decline CDW when offered — your protection is already in place.
Phase 2 — At the Rental Counter
4
Decline CDW at the Counter

When rental staff offer CDW or LDW insurance, politely say: "No thank you, I have my own cover." This is legal and you're entitled to do it.

On a 7-day rental, this single act saves you up to $245 vs paying $35/day at the desk.
5
Pick Up Your Car & Drive

The rental company will hold a deposit on your credit card — this is standard. If no damage occurs, it's released at return. You're covered from your policy start date.

Phase 3 — If Something Goes Wrong
6
Document Everything Immediately

If there's damage or theft, photograph everything immediately. The rental company will assess the damage and may charge your credit card. Keep all receipts and correspondence.

7
Submit Your Claim Online

Log in, start a claim, and upload your documents. You'll need:

  • Rental Agreement (full document)
  • Damage Invoice or Receipt
  • Driver Licence (all drivers)
  • Photos of Damage (min. 3)
8
Get Reimbursed

We acknowledge your claim within 24 hours. Once approved, reimbursement is processed to your bank account within 3–5 business days. Your out-of-pocket cost: $0.

What We Reimburse
  • Repair costs up to your coverage limit
  • Key replacement fees
  • Towing costs
  • Admin & processing charges
  • Windscreen & glass
  • Tyre damage
What's Not Covered
  • Drink driving incidents
  • Off-road use (base plans)
  • Personal belongings
  • Mechanical failures
  • Interior misuse damage
