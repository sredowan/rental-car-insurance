Insider Knowledge

What the rental company won't tell you

CDW doesn't cover everything

Windscreen cracks, tyre damage, admin fees and processing charges are often excluded from the rental desk CDW — even after you've paid for it.

Your credit card may not protect you

Most credit card cover is secondary — you'd need to claim through your personal auto insurance first, which can raise your premiums.

You can say NO at the counter

If you have your own independent policy, you're legally protected. Simply decline the counter upsell and save yourself hundreds of dollars.
