🛡️ About Coverage
Car hire excess insurance covers the excess (deductible) charged by a rental company if their car is damaged or stolen while in your care. Without this cover, you could be liable for thousands of dollars in repair costs, admin fees, and loss of use charges. Rental Sheild reimburses you so your out-of-pocket cost is $0.
All plans cover: Collision damage · Theft · Windscreen & glass · Tyre damage · Undercarriage damage · Key replacement & lockout · Admin & processing fees · Towing costs · All authorized drivers on the rental agreement. All with $0 excess — you pay nothing when you claim.
