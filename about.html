Our Mission

We exist to end
rental desk overcharging.

Every year, Australian car renters are overcharged millions of dollars at rental counters for insurance they could get for half the price.

Rental Sheild was founded with a single mission: to give every car renter access to the same protection at a fair, transparent price — before they ever reach the rental counter.

When you pick up a rental car, you're often rushed, tired, and unfamiliar with the paperwork. Counter staff are trained to capitalise on that. We're here to change the equation — giving you the information and protection you need, ahead of time, at a price that's simply fair.
