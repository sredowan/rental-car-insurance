Details

Full Coverage Breakdown

Covered on All Plans
  • Collision damage – Up to your chosen limit
  • Theft – If the vehicle is stolen
  • Windscreen & glass – Including chips and cracks
  • Tyre damage – Punctures and blowouts
  • Undercarriage damage
  • Key replacement & lockout
  • Admin & processing fees – Often excluded elsewhere
  • Towing costs – After an accident
  • All authorized drivers – Everyone on the rental agreement
  • $0 excess – You pay nothing when you claim
Not Covered
  • Interior damage from misuse – Spills, burns, interior vandalism
  • Driving under the influence
  • Off-road use – Without specific add-on
  • Personal belongings – Luggage, electronics left in car
  • Mechanical failure – Not accident-related
  • Racing or competitive use
  • Private vehicle rentals – e.g. peer-to-peer platforms
